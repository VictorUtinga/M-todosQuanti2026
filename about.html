About Me

Biography

I am a Master's student in Political Science at the Faculty of Philosophy, Letters and Human Sciences of the University of São Paulo (FFLCH-USP), under the supervision of Professor Catarina Ianni Segatto.

I hold a B.A. in Social Sciences from the University of São Paulo (USP). During my undergraduate studies, I conducted research funded by the São Paulo Research Foundation (FAPESP), focusing on educational implementation processes.

My academic agenda is centered on understanding how public policies—specifically in the education sector—are translated from legislation into practice, considering the role of bureaucracy and the institutional constraints within the Brazilian federation.
