Welcome

Welcome to my academic website. I am a researcher in Political Science at the University of São Paulo, specializing in Public Policy, Education, and Bureaucracy.

My work currently focuses on the implementation of educational policies in Brazil, with a specific interest in the "Novo Ensino Médio" reform and its impacts on the teaching career and professional development.
