Current Research

Teaching career and continuing education in the implementation of the New High School: a comparison between São Paulo, Ceará and Pernambuco.

This research investigates the implementation of the New High School (Novo Ensino Médio) reform in Brazil by analyzing the changes adopted by state governments in teacher career structures and continuing education policies.

The study utilizes a qualitative approach, including the analysis of official documents, legislation, and implementation plans, complemented by semi-structured interviews with policymakers, school administrators, and teachers.

Principal Investigator: Eduardo Cesar Leão Marques
